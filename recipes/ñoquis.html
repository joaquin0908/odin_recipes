Ñoquis

Ñoquis is assembled and baked in an oven until hot and melted. The dish is typically cut into square or rectangular portions for serving.

  • 500 g minced meat (mixture of pork and beef)
  • 1 onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 celery
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 800 g crushed tomato
  • 200 ml red wine
  • Olive Oil
  • Herbs (oregano, basil) to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare the Bolognese Sauce: 1.1. Finely chop the onion, carrot, celery and garlic. 1.2. In a large frying pan, heat the olive oil and sauté the vegetables until tender. 1.3. Add the minced meat and cook until browned. 1.4. Deglaze with the red wine and let it reduce. 1.5. Add the crushed tomato, salt, pepper and herbs. Simmer for 30-40 minutes.
  2. Assembling the Lasagna: 3.1. Preheat the oven to 180°C. 3.2. In a baking dish, place a layer of Bolognese sauce. 3.3. Place a layer of lasagna sheets on top. 3.4. Add a layer of béchamel sauce. 3.5. Repeat the layers until you finish with a layer of béchamel. 3.6. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese and grated Parmesan on top.
  3. Baking: 4.1. Bake for 30-40 minutes, until the surface is golden brown and bubbly. 4.2. Let it rest for a few minutes before serving.
